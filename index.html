A Cryptographic Hash-Chaining Framework for Real-Time Tamper-Evidence in Live Video Streams

Karan • Founder, Entangle • krkaranraj.me@gmail.com

Generative AI has made verifying live video evidence critical. This paper introduces a cryptographic evidence pipeline combining frame-level SHA-256 hashing, DCT/wavelet perceptual hashing (pHash), Merkle tree aggregation, and hardware enclave signatures to deliver deterministic, real-time tamper-evidence.

⚡ Dual Hash Pipeline: Bit-exact SHA-256 + Perceptual pHash against deepfakes & frame splicing.
🔗 Frame Chaining: H_i = SHA256(H_{i-1} || c_i || p_i || M_i) for sequential integrity.
🛡️ Hardware Trust: Merkle root signing via Android StrongBox TEE & iOS Secure Enclave.
